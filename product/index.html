coffee pack & coffee banking

Now you can easily enjoy specialty coffee with various characters, aromas, and flavors from different coffee-producing regions in Indonesia available at the nearest Coffee Toffee outlets.

COFFEE PACK

There are several single-origin Arabica specialties with unique aromas, flavors, and characters from various regions that you can order at Coffee Toffee outlets to enjoy at home or simply as a gift for your family/relatives/friends. Among them are: Bali Kintamani, Sumatra Linthong, Bali Batukaru, Sumatra Gayo, Java Mocha, Mount Malabar, and Sulawesi Toraja.

LIMITED STOCK

One of the highly anticipated single-origin specialties by coffee lovers. Rare & limited stock specialty coffee beans that are only available at certain times, ranging from peaberry coffee, civet coffee, liberica, maragogype, to single-origin beans specially processed by experts from upstream to downstream, used as the flagship in barista competitions.

COFFEE BANKING

For you black coffee enthusiasts, we offer coffee banking. By purchasing 1 pack of single-origin specialty (coffee banking), you can deposit the coffee pack with the barista at our outlet. It's super simple: when you come to the store, just mention your name and order to the barista, and your black coffee will be freshly brewed and served to you immediately, completely free of charge, no additional fees whatsoever.
