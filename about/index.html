our story

We are a coffee company that dedicates all our passion, love, and enthusiasm to the amazing world of coffee. We are a local company, and most of the raw materials we use are local as well. Yes, and we say that proudly. We know, just like you do, that the best coffee beans in the world come from Indonesia.

We travel across various parts of Indonesia and collaborate with local coffee farmers and roasters to acquire the best taste profiles of Indonesian coffee, from grade one Arabica coffee beans to various plantations scattered throughout the archipelago.

With our experience and knowledge in the retail coffee industry, from sourcing coffee beans to designing a coffee bar, we make your coffee business journey EASY, SIMPLE, and FUN!

We offer a fun and lifestyle-oriented business to those who share the same passion and love for the world of coffee as we do
